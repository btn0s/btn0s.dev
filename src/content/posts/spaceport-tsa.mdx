# Spaceport TSA

A game where you and your friends are TSA agents at a spaceport.

## Concept

The game is a cooperative multiplayer game where you and your friends are TSA agents at a spaceport. You have to work together to scan passengers and their luggage to ensure the safety of the spaceport.

## Gameplay

The game is played in rounds. Each round, passengers will arrive at the spaceport and you have to scan them and their luggage. You have to work together to scan the passengers and their luggage to ensure the safety of the spaceport.
